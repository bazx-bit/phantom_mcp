Why no separate textures?

Coffee-Tech uses a self-contained GLB — all textures (BaseColor, Normal, Metallic, Roughness) are baked directly inside the .glb binary. This is different from the StringTune katana, which loads 10 separate texture files externally. Both approaches work, but self-contained GLBs are simpler to rip since everything comes in one file.
